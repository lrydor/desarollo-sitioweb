The Great Blue Hole

The Great Blue Hole is a giant marine sinkhole off the coast of Belize. Located near the center of Lighthouse Reef, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most stunning diving spots in the world. Its circular shape and deep blue color make it a natural wonder, attracting divers and scientists alike.
